Chao Zhang  —  Short Biography

Experience

Chao Zhang is now a Ph.D. student with the Department of Control and Systems Engineering, Nanjing University (NJU), under the supervision of Professor Chunlin Chen. Before the Ph.D. career, he received the M.S. degree in Control Science and Engineering in 2021, and the B.S. degree in Automation in 2018 from NJU.
