Biography

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Control Science and Intelligence Engineering, Nanjing University (NJU), under the supervision of Prof. Chunlin Chen. I received the M.S. degree in Control Science and Engineering in 2021, and the B.S. degree in Automation in 2018 from NJU.

My research interests include multi-view/modal learning, representation learning, un/semi/weakly-supervised learning.
