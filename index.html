About me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Control Science and Intelligence Engineering, Nanjing University (NJU), under the supervision of Prof. Chunlin Chen and Prof. Huaxiong Li. Before the Ph.D. career, I received the M.S. degree in Control Science and Engineering in 2021, and the B.S. degree in Automation in 2018 from NJU.

My research interests include multi-view/modal learning, representation learning, unsupervised learning.
