Biography

My main research interests lie in machine learning and pattern recognition, including multi-modal/view learning, representation learning, un/semi/weakly-supervised learning, online learning, as well as their related applications. I got my Ph.D., M.E., and B.E. degrees from the Department of Control Science and Engineering at Nanjing University in 2025, 2021, and 2018, respectively.
